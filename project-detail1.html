Client

Benelli bicycle

Concept

The Foldable e-bike offers an easy, smooth and active user experience. Folding and unfolding the bicycle in less than ten seconds lets riders adapt to their environment. The Foldable e-bike is lighter, smaller and even more accessible product to inspire new riders with new ways to ride. We included the battery inside of its frame for its safety and to protect it from the rain. We developed 3 kinds of capacity for different purposes.
